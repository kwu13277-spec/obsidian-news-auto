Five of the seven villagers who became trapped in a cave that flooded in Laos were found alive by rescuers Wednesday, in an incident echoing the dramatic 2018 rescue of teenage soccer players in Thailand.

周三，救援人员发现七名因洞穴被淹而受困的老挝村民中有五人仍然活着，这起事件让人联想到2018年泰国少年足球队那次戏剧性的救援行动。

They were located by specialist cave divers and for now remain stuck in an underground cavern in the central Laotian province of Xaisomboun, as rescuers continue to search for the two remaining people.

这些村民是由专业洞穴潜水员定位的，目前他们仍被困在老挝中部赛宋奔省的一个地下洞穴中，救援人员继续搜寻剩余的两名人员。

“5 people have been found safe. The search continues for the remaining 2,” Thai rescue diver Kengkad Bongkawong wrote on Facebook at 4.30 p.m. local time.

“5人已被发现安全。剩余2人的搜寻仍在继续，”泰国救援潜水员Kengkad Bongkawong于当地时间下午4点30分在Facebook上写道。

A Laotian rescue group, Rescue Volunteer for People, said that the five found were “alive and all safe.”

一个名为“老挝人民救援志愿者”的救援组织表示，被发现的五人“都活着，而且全部安全”。

Rescue teams working above ground were seen jumping for joy, hugging one other and crying when they learned that five people had been found alive, in scenes posted on social media.

社交媒体上发布的画面显示，地面上的救援团队在得知五人被找到的消息后，欢呼雀跃，互相拥抱并流下眼泪。

Finnish diver Mikko Paasi, who is part of the rescue operation, expressed his happiness at locating five of the trapped. “The task so far has been far from easy and everybody involved has done amazing work,” he wrote on Instagram. ...

参与救援行动的芬兰潜水员Mikko Paasi表达了对找到五人后的喜悦之情。“到目前为止，这项任务远非易事，每个参与其中的人都付出了惊人的努力，”他在Instagram上写道。

He added, however, that it was only from the “short comfort” as the survivors remain trapped for now. “They are all healthy, good mental state, but the evacuation is ahead, and it will not be easy,” he said.

不过，他补充说这仅仅是“短暂的安慰”，因为幸存者目前仍被困在洞穴中。“所有人都健康，精神状况良好，但撤离工作仍在前面，而且绝对不会轻松，”他说。

He and another diver are now tasked with “going back down straight away” to deliver more supplies to the villagers to help them regain their strength for the journey out of the cave.

他和另一名潜水员当前的任务是“立即再次下潜”，给村民们送去更多补给，以便他们恢复体力，为走出洞穴做好准备。
